About Silent Signals

Making the invisible wounds of emotional abuse visible through technology

Silent Signals was born from a deeply personal observation — that emotional abuse doesn't always leave visible scars, but its damage runs just as deep. Coming from a background where silence was often mistaken for peace, I realized how many people suffer through manipulation, gaslighting, bullying, and other forms of verbal abuse without ever being heard or validated.

Our Mission

This project began as a mission to give voice to the unspoken. Using natural language processing (NLP) techniques like sentiment analysis and keyword recognition, Silent Signals scans conversations and identifies subtle patterns of emotional abuse.

It doesn't just classify—it educates, breaking down the nature of abuse into clear categories so users can understand what's happening and why it's not okay.

How We Help

Detect

Our algorithms analyze text for patterns of gaslighting, manipulation, bullying, and other abusive behaviors.

Educate

We explain why certain phrases are harmful, helping users recognize abuse they might have missed.

Empower

We provide resources and guidance to help users take next steps toward healthier relationships.

Why This Matters

Whether it's a toxic friendship, a controlling relationship, or online bullying, Silent Signals is a step towards awareness and action. By making emotional abuse visible through tech, this project empowers users to recognize red flags early and seek help.

Built with empathy. Backed by code. Aimed at healing.

Not all wounds are visible. But now, their signals are.

Our Promise

Silent Signals is committed to:

  • Providing accurate, judgment-free analysis
  • Keeping your data private and secure
  • Continuously improving our detection methods
  • Offering compassionate support resources
