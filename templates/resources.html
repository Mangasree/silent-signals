Resources

Find help and support to deal with emotional abuse

Helplines

Immediate support is available through these services:

  • Women Helpline – 181. Government-run support for women in distress, including emotional/verbal abuse.
  • National Commission for Women (NCW) Helpline – 7827170170 WhatsApp-based complaint service for harassment, abuse, or emotional violence.

Support Networks

Reach out to these support systems:

  • Trusted family members or friends
  • Local community support groups
  • Professional counselors or therapists

Self-Care Tips

Maintain your mental health:

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation
  • Maintain a journal of your feelings
  • Set healthy boundaries in relationships
  • Engage in physical activity regularly
